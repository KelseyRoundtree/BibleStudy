Bible Study

Using the SOAP method

"What we write down is what we remember. It’s like a time capsule in a way, a lifeline back to the best parts of ourselves. A little popcorn trail of words we can follow so that we never lose sight of the path we’re on."
Allison Fallon

  • S
    Scripture:
    Write out which bible verse you want to unpack! Writing down the verse will help to memorize what you are studying.
  • O
    Observation:
    While reading the verse, is there anything that stands out to you? What do you think the message of the verse is?
  • A
    Application;
    In what ways does the message of this verse apply to your life? What can you change?
  • P
    Prayer:
    If there is something that you feel God has revealed to you, pray about it! Ask the Lord to give you the strength to apply the message of the verse to your life.
